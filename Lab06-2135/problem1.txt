Foundation in Information Technology

In an ever-changing, technologically dependent world, our one-year Foundation in Information Technology programme
aims to produce students who are well-equipped with computer skills as well as mathematical and physics skills. 
The Foundation in Information Technology programme is delivered through engaging lectures and laboratory work 
which serve to build knowledge and help develop practical skills.

After completion of the foundation programme you can opt for a degree programme 
from either Faculty of Computing and Informatics (FCI) or Faculty of Information, Science and Technology (FIST).

Entry Requirements

Pass SPM/O-Level or its equivalent with a minimum of Grade C in at least five (5) subjects inclusive of Mathematics and English; OR
Pass UEC with a minimum of Grade B in at least three (3) subjects inclusive of Mathematics and English.

Additional Requirement to pursue Bachelor of Computer Science (Honours):

A Credit in Additional Mathematics at SPM Level or its equivalent; OR
Credit in Mathematics AND one Science/Technology/Engineering subject at SPM Level or its equivalent.
